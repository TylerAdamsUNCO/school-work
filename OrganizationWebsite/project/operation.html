Operations and Missions

Team Rocket has carried out many secret missions and operations!

  • Rocket Game Corner

    • The Rocket Game Corner is in Celadon City. We sell stolen Pokémon to unsuspecting trainers after they’ve wasted all their money on coins for slots. This all funds Team Rocket! We also have a secret base under the Game Corner with sliding tiles and a lift. Just don't drop the Lift Key.

  • Mt Moon Excavation

    • Team Rocket delved into Mt Moon to look for Rare Fossils! Unfortunately we were beaten to the punch by some Super Nerd, but we did capture some rare Pokémon like Clefairy.

  • Pokémon Tower

    • In pursuit of Cubone skulls, Team Rocket infiltrated Pokémon Tower in Lavender Town while everyone was cowering from ghosts.

  • Silph Co Infiltration

    • Team Rocket infiltrated the Silph Company to obtain the ultimate Pokéball, the Masterball. The Masterball can capture any Pokémon without fail, so it seems only fitting that Team Rocket should have it. The operation was led by Giovanni himself, and we captured the president of Silph Co to hold him hostage. Also on the mission were the esteemed Four Rocket Brothers, who were stationed throughout the building.
